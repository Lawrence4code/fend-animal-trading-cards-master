Lhasa Apso - Rusty

Lhasa apso is one of the best dog breed, originated in Tibet they are domestic and non-sporting breed.

  • Lhasa Apso Means: Long Hair Lhasa Dog
  • Average Weight: 8kg
  • Average Height: 12 inches
  • Life Span: 14 - 16 years
  • Dog Color: All colors

Lhasa apso are fun loving dogs, very loyal and close to their owner and they are not friendly to strangers and do take time to get acquainted but they are surely harmless.

Dogs are my favourite animal and I am writing this piece on Lhasa Apso because my buddy Rusty is a Lhasa Apso Breed.
